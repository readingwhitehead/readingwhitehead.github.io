Secondary Sources for Understanding Whitehead's Process and Reality

Acknowledement: This list consists of free online materials. I would like to thank the scholars, publishers, and institutions who have made these resources openly available.
Last updated: March 2026. This page is updated occasionally but not on a regular schedule. Some external links may stop working over time, and I do not actively check them.

1. General Sources

Wordbook
  • Cobb, John B., Jr. Whitehead Word Book: A Glossary with Alphabetical Index to Technical Terms in Process and Reality. Cleveland, OH: Process and Faith P, 2008 (available on The Information Philosopher).
Reading Companions
  • Kraus, Elizabeth M. The Metaphysics of Experience: A Companion to Whitehead’s Process and Reality. Fordham University Press, 1998.
  • Sherburne, Donald W. A Key to Whitehead’s Process and Reality. London: Collier-Macmillan, 1966.
General Introductions
  • Lowe, Victor. Understanding Whitehead. Baltimore, MD: Johns Hopkins University Press, 2019. Open access edition. Originally published 1962.
  • Leclerc, Ivor. Whitehead's Metaphysics: An Introductory Exposition. London: Allen and Unwin; New York: Macmillan, 1958.
  • Christian, William A. An Interpretation of Whitehead's Metaphysics. New Haven: Yale University Press, 1959.
  • Hartshorne, Charles. Whitehead's Philosophy: Selected Essays, 1935-1970. Lincoln: University of Nebraska Press, 1972.
*The more comprehensive list of introductions to Whitehead can be found in the Centre of Process Studies Thematic Bibliography .
The list is available here (Centre of Process Studies's Google Drive) .

Teaching
  • Ford, Lewis S. “A Guide to Whitehead.” Teaching Philosophy 7, no. 2 (April 1984): 143–147.
  • Educator’s Toolbox. Centre of Process Studies.
Videos (Reading Aids)
  • McDaniel, J. (n.d.). Whitehead videos. Open Horizons.
  • Footnotes2Plato. (n.d.). Lectures on Whitehead's "Process & Reality" [YouTube playlist].
Videos (General Introductions)
  • Kramer Knipe. (2017, July 24). Alfred North Whitehead’s Process Metaphysics. YouTube.
  • Wheaton College. (n.d.). Arthur Holmes: A History of Philosophy (Lecture 61 to 63 on Process Philosophy) [YouTube playlist].
  • Footnotes2Plato. (2024, Dec 17). Why the World is Unfinished: Whitehead in 20 Minutes YouTube.
Encyclopedia Article
  • Hustwit, J. R. (n.d.). “Process Philosophy,” *Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy*.
Topic-Indexed Guide
  • Tanaka, Yutaka. The Contextual Index of Process and Reality. Last updated March 17, 1996. The Japan Society for Process Studies (Sophia University). Online resource.

  • McHenry, Leemon B. "Whitehead's panpsychism as the subjectivity of prehension" Process Studies 24, 1995, pp. 1-14.
  • Wikipedia contributors. "Prehension (Philosophy)." Wikipedia. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prehension_(philosophy)
God
  • Lansing, John W. "The ‘Natures’ of Whitehead’s God". Process Studies Vol. 3, Number 3, Fall, 1973, pp. 143-152.
